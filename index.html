Welcome to JDM Imports

We import and provide the best JDM cars from Japan to enthusiasts worldwide. Explore our collection and services tailored to your passion for unique imports.

The Toyota AE86, or Hachiroku, is an iconic 1980s lightweight, rear-wheel-drive sports car. Known for its role in drifting and the anime "Initial D", its a symbol of JDM culture and loved for its agile handling

The Toyota GR86 is the modern successor to the AE86. With a 2.4L engine, rear-wheel drive, and lightweight design, it offers a thrilling, driver-focused experience, blending nostalgia with modern performance.
